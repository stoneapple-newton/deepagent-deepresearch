# Research Plan: Evaluating ASR Transcripts Without Ground Truth

**Date:** 2026-06-24
**Target audience:** Developer/practitioner building an ASR evaluation pipeline who lacks reference transcripts.

## Sub-questions

1. **Core methods** - What are the established approaches for reference-free (unsupervised) ASR transcript evaluation?
2. **Existing tools** - What libraries, APIs, and open-source evaluators exist that can be used directly?
3. **Latest research** - What are the most recent developments (2024-2026) in this space?
4. **Trade-offs & best practices** - What are the limitations, when does each method work/fail, and what are recommended practices?

## Expected report shape

- Executive summary
- Problem background: why reference-free evaluation matters
- Categories of approaches (confidence-based, LM-based, acoustic, self-supervised, ensemble)
- Survey of existing tools and evaluators
- Recent research highlights
- Comparison / decision guide
- Limitations and open questions
- Sources
